17世纪，巴黎一个蛋糕师与人打赌，要做一个100层的蛋糕。尽管，最终
没有记载到底做了多少层，但是这款蛋糕后来有了一个响亮的名字“拿破
仑”。而拿破仑蛋糕的法文为Mille feuille，即100万层酥皮的意思。

Curiously, the Napoleon has been adopted as the unofficial national cake of Russia. Napoleon invaded Russia in 1812. Yet it appears all is forgiven when the moniker adorns custard and pastry. In fact, the cake predates Napoleon himself, the newer name thought to be sycophantic tweaking of Neopolitain, or ‘of Naples’. The true origins of the Napoleon are lost to history, though it’s likely the Napoleon would have graced the tables of royalty from ancient times. Times change, but taste is permanent.
